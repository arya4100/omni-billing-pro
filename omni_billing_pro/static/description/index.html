OmniBilling Pro — All-in-One Business Billing

OmniBilling Pro is a classic, fully-featured business billing and payment tracking module for Odoo 16. Designed for businesses of all sizes, it centralises your invoices, transactions, and payment management in one clean and premium interface — no third-party dependencies required.

⭐ Key Features

📄 Professional Invoicing

Create and manage customised business invoices with auto-generated sequential reference numbers (e.g. INV/2026/00001), due dates, multiple line items, and terms fields.

💳 Payment Tracking

Register payments against any open invoice — full or partial. Supports Cash, Bank Transfer, Credit Card, and Online payments. Each transaction gets its own reference (e.g. TRX/2026/00001).

🔄 Automatic Balance Calculation

The system automatically calculates Total Amount, Amount Paid, and Amount Due in real time. Invoices auto-transition to Paid status once the balance hits zero.

💬 Built-in Chatter & Activity Tracking

Every invoice and transaction includes Odoo's native mail thread, activity scheduling, and follower management — giving you a full audit trail for every financial document.

🔐 Role-based Access Control

All internal Odoo users have full access to create and manage invoices and transactions — ready to use out-of-the-box with no extra configuration.

🏢 Multi-currency Ready

Uses your company's default currency, making it compatible with any Odoo localization and multi-currency setup.

📸 Screenshots

Invoice List View

A clean, filterable list of all your business invoices at a glance. Color-coded status badges make it easy to spot what needs attention.



Invoice Form — Create & Confirm

Add multiple line items with descriptions, quantities and unit prices. Auto-calculated subtotals and totals. Status bar guides users from Draft → Open → Paid.



Transaction Management

A dedicated transactions log showing every payment event across your business, with method, amount, linked invoice, and status clearly visible.
